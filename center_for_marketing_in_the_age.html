Dr. Samira Raki

Director
Center for Marketing in the Age of Sustainable Development

University of Sustainable Development (USD)

Dr. Samira Raki, A Moroccan Scholar, holds the leadership position as the Director of this center, Center for Marketing in the Age of Sustainable Development. In Addition to this position, she has been teaching as an Associate Professor of the EMAA Business School based in Agadir, Morocco.

She received her PhD degree in Marketing from the Universiti Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS), Sarawak, Malaysia. Before attending PhD degree, She completed Master of Science in Accounting, Control and Auditing from the Rennes I University, Rennes, France and Bachelor Degree in Computerized Methods Applied to Management from Paris XII University, Paris, France.

Academic Awards and Scholarship:
  • Winner of “Academic Excellence 2020” from Faculty of Business and Economics, UNIMAS.
  • Winner of “Best Researcher Award” in Organizational dynamics, Knowledge, and Business Strategies, and “Best Researcher (Overall) Award” at BBRC, 2018.
  • Winner of “Best Paper Award” at BBRC, 2016.
  • ZAMALAH Scholarship, Universiti Malaysia Sarawak (UNIMAS), February 2016 - January 2019.
Membership:

  • Member of the Editorial Board of the Moroccan Journal of Business Studies from Oct. 2020-now.
  • Member of the organizing and scientific committee of the International Colloquiums held by EMAA BSchool from Oct. 2020-now.
  • Review Board Member of the Journal of Innovative research in Management Sciences (Pakistan) from 2021-now.

About Us
