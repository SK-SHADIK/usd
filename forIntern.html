Internship Opportunity

Time-Length of the Internship:

The length of the internship varies from a few weeks, to half a year or longer. There is no strict rule for the duration of a placement. We tailor to the need of the intern, the ongoing research and programme. However, USD recommends an engagement of minimum of 3 - 4 months to make the experience beneficial for both the intern and the organization.


Eligibility:

USD enrolls candidates who possess a passion to learn and contribute to the field of development. We strongly feel that educational or professional background should not limit any potential candidate from applying. Eligibility criteria are flexible to accommodate interns regardless of their curricular and professional experiences. The primary requirements for the Internship Programme are:

  • Students accepted into an undergraduate program in a college or university
  • Students enrolled in an undergraduate / graduate program in an accredited academic institution with good CGPA
  • PhD students (national or international) pursuing thesis at a host institution
  • Some experience in the field of development sector like education, health, SDGs can be an asset including experience in research in social sciences and development related programmes
  • Good command in written and spoken English
  • Some knowledge on research methodologies including operating data analysis tools for qualitative and quantitative research
  • Applicants must demonstrate an interest in development sector

Special Circumstances

High School graduates aiming to pursue higher studies in public health can engaged in ongoing research project(s) based on an assessment that they will have valuable contributions to organisational initiatives.


If candidate's research supports a senior Faculty's ongoing projects on a short or long term.


Note: USD's internship programme does NOT provide any compensation and/or salary for the contribution of the candidate.


Requirements for Enrollment:

To carry out the assigned duties and responsibilities and report to his/her manager accordingly

Certification

Interns will receive a certificate and an appreciation letter from the manager and the Director Academic Affairs and the Vice-Chancellor of the University upon successful completion of the internship.
